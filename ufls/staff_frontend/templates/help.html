Staff Help

Accessing the Furrydelphia Staff Portal.

For Staff Before 2021

In May 2021, we sent out a form for staff to fill out which gave you a staff account e-mail (e.g. fluffy@staff.furrydelphia.org). You must use this e-mail to login to the staff portal.


For Staff After 2021

You will have recieved an onboarding e-mail from ufls@furrydelphia.org containing your Staff E-mail, Temp Password, and Instructions for Logging In. If you've forgotten your password, you can use the Google Password Reset Form. Your recovery e-mail is the e-mail provided to us in your application. If you still run into any issues, you can e-mail cto@furrydelphia.org for assistance.


For Volunteers

Volunteers do not have access to this staff portal. You have a unique portal for which you can manage your volunteering hours and other information. Please check your e-mail for more details.
