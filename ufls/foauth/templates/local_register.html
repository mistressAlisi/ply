What is UFLS?

UFLS is the Unified Furrydelphia Login System which allows Attendees and Staff to use the same login credentials across multiple Furrydelphia Applications and Services. With UFLS, you can manage your Convention Profile across years without needing to sign up over and over again.

Use UFLS to:

  • Register for Furrydelphia
  • Manage your Submitted Panels and Events
  • Manage and submit Applications for Dealers, Artists Alley, DJs, and More
  • Manage your Dealers Den Profile
